Research Interests:

  • Signaling Games

Lewis-Skyrms signaling game models can be used to think about language evolution and acquisition as well as low level symbolic reasoning.

  • Epistemic Networks

Epistemic network models are used in a branch of formal social epistemology that investigates how networks of agents gather and share information

  • Cultural Evolution

I've coauthored a paper on racial capitalism using a Nash bargaining game. Recently, I've been working on complex social identity signaling in a coordination game.
